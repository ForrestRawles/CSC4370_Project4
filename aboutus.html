Project Leader

Forrest Rawles

Responsibilities:
Hello, my name is Forrest Rawles, and I hope your having a great day. I work on 2 main portions of the website; the user registration and the seller section. The user registration is a very important aspect for any website, where all the information must be stored properly. The seller pages require the user to have an ui that shows all the property that they are selling or an add button. The add button redirects the user to a page that lets them put their property information.

How Was Scrum Beneficial to You During this Project?

Scrum helped us organized key portions of the project that we needed to do in a short amount of time. There is so much going on, so being able to work on sections and talk to each other when we needed help worked well in the long run. Each time we meet up, we had ideas to each other's problem, so it made our team more efficent. There were a lot of bugs, and being able to talk and have others give their perspective was a huge benifit. For the future, SCRUM would be really useful for just programming in big teams. There could be big projects where the deadline is in a short amount of time, and SCRUM could help spread the workload effiecently to each programmer on the team. It would lead to a bit less stress in the working enviorment.

How Was Scrum Beneficial to You During this Project?

Keeping up with my group members using the Scrum methodology was really the only way I was able to keep myself flexible and cover so many parts of the project. By having daily meetings with my team, I was really able to see what my team members were working on, and find quick places to fill in to help get the project built. It also helped me solve a bunch of problems easily by mentioning them to my team on the daily and us being able to have quick solutions ready to fix items that were not working properly such as the login page logging in all users as admins at one point. I believe Scrum will be beneficial to me in the fiture as it will allow me to get quick answers to my questions inside the daily meeting format and also allow me to find spaces quickly and easily that need help so I can make sure that our site in the future is fully flushed out.
